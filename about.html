About WattBuddy

WattBuddy is a simple website designed to help users understand and manage their household energy use without feeling overwhelmed or confused.

The goal of this project is to present useful household usage information in a clear, friendly, and easy-to-follow way.

The Problem

Many households want to reduce energy use, but the information can often feel confusing, technical, or difficult to act on. Users may not know where to start, which habits matter most, or how small changes can make a difference.

This website focuses on making household usage easier to understand by breaking information into clear sections, simple tips, and practical tools.

Our Solution

Our solution is a user-friendly website that gives households a central place to view information, learn tips, track usage, and set simple goals.

Instead of showing too much information at once, the website separates content into different pages so users can explore at their own pace.

How This Helps Users

The website is designed to support users by using simple language, clear navigation, and focused pages. This helps reduce confusion and makes the information easier to use.

By guiding users through small actions and easy-to-understand information, the website encourages better household habits without making users feel pressured.
